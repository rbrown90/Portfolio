ABOUT ME

Hello again! I am Rakin Brown, a full stack engineer. Since I have been a wee toddler, I always had a love for innovation and building things. I always figured I wanted to be some type of engineer... So I decided to combine my love for engineering, the web, and computers. I started off my career teaching myself SQL, and felt like I could make certain applications better...if I knew how they worked. So I started ordering books and soon enough found myself lost in the rabbit hole of developing software. I spent countless hours and weeks, trying and failing to make what I wanted to see in my system work. Though it was challenging, it eventually worked. I then had the realization that this is what I loved doing. I changed my major in school, bought more books, and even signed up for a bootcamp. It's safe to say I'm hooked. I wish to use my skills and knowledge to continue to create. Speaking of create, check out some of my awesome work!
